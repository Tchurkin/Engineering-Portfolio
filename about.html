About

Student aerospace engineer. Controls, embedded systems, and hardware that gets tested for real.

About Me

I build and test closed-loop control systems on real model aerospace hardware. My work centers on thrust vector control rockets, where I integrate mechanical systems, embedded electronics, and control algorithms into one flight system.

Across more than 15 launches, I use failure-driven iteration to refine controller stability, structural design, and recovery systems. I build systems that can be rapidly tested, analyzed, and improved.
