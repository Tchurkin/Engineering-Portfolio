Sisyphus Development

Landing-focused architecture from first principles to integrated testing. Trajectory control, autonomy logic, and descent systems integration.

Modeled after the clearer project deep-dives on AeroFusion, this page breaks Sisyphus into phased development chunks with visible progress markers and evidence links.

Roadmap System Architecture Test Plan Evidence Links

Roadmap

Stage 01

Ascent and State Backbone

Define flight-state transitions, estimator inputs, and critical telemetry needed for landing decisions.

Stage 02

Descent Guidance Logic

Build and validate descent trajectory shaping with fuel-margin and attitude constraints.

Stage 03

Landing Burn Controller

Implement coupled vertical/lateral control with thrust-vector authority and event timing checks.

Stage 04

Autonomous Recovery Logic

Integrate fallback triggers: cutoff criteria, leg deployment window, and parachute contingency mode.

System Architecture

Flight Modes

Prelaunch, boost, coast, re-ignition, terminal descent, and touchdown verification states.

Estimator Stack

Multi-sensor fusion strategy to estimate position, vertical velocity, and attitude under noisy dynamics.

Control Allocation

Distribute control effort across thrust vectoring and event scheduling without violating actuator limits.

Safety Layer

Abort/fallback logic gates for scenarios where descent solution quality falls below thresholds.
